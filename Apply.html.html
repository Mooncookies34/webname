How to apply

**Fill out the Application Form:**

Start by filling out our job application form. Make sure to provide accurate and complete information. The following details are required:

- **Full Name:** Enter your full name as it appears on your official documents.

- **Email:** Provide a valid email address for communication.

- **Phone Number:** Enter an active phone number where we can reach you.

- **Resume:** Upload your resume in PDF, DOC, or DOCX format.

- **Position Applied For:** Specify the job title you are interested in.

- **Years of Experience:** Mention the number of years of relevant experience you have.

- **Cover Letter:** Write a cover letter introducing yourself and explaining your interest in the position. This is your opportunity to showcase your qualifications and motivation.

2. **Submit Your Application:** After completing all the required fields, click the "Submit Application" button at the bottom of the form. Your application will be sent to our recruitment team for review.
